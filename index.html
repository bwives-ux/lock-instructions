Instructions to create a unique private code for your personal private door lock

MANUFACTURERS OPERATING INSTRUCTIONS:

(This lock is pre-set at the factory, to operate/open at 0-0-0)

1) Step (1)

a) Turn dials to 0-0-0 (Factory setting), to open door for first time.

b) and pull out the shackle:

2) Step (2)

a) Turn the shackle counterclockwise 90°

b) and press it down:

3) Step (3)

a) While holding shackle down,

b) Rotate it a further 90°.

4) Step (4)

a) Create a new, private secret code and turn the dials to this position (in this demonstration, the secret code is 5-5-5)

b) Keep a record of the new combination.

5) Step (5)

a) Rotate the shackle 90° clockwise backward to the first 90° rotation position.

b) Slightly pull to raise the shackle

c) Turn the shackle further clockwise 90° to its original position.

d) Close the shackle.

The lock will no longer open at 0-0-0, instead will open only with your new secret code, close the shackle and rotate dials randomly to keep the lock closed.

Please reset combination to 0-0-0 when vacating
