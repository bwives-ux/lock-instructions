Instructions to create a private code for your door lock

 

MANUFACTURERS OPERATING INSTRUCTIONS

This lock is pre-set at the factory to operate/open at 0-0-0

1.          Turn dials to 0-0-0, and pull out shackle.

2.          Turn shackle counter-clockwise 90 and press it down.

3.          While holding shackle down, rotate it a further 90.

4.          Now change the dial to your own secret code, do not leave code at 0-0-0 as that is the factory reset position.

5.          Turn the shackle clockwise back to the original 90 position.

6.          The lock will no longer open at 0-0-0, instead will open only with your new secret code, close the shackle and rotate dials randomly to keep the lock closed.

 

Please reset combination to 0-0-0 when vacating

 

FOLLOWING IS AN INSTRUCTIONAL GUIDE DETAILING EACH STEP IN THE PROCESS OF CHANGING YOUR DOOR CODE. PLEASE DONT FORGET YOUR CODE!

1)                   Step (1)

a)                 


Turn dials to 0-0-0 (Factory setting), to open door for first time.

2)                   Step (2)

a)                  Turn the shackle counterclockwise 90

b)                  and press it down:

 

3)                   Step (3)

a)                  While holding shackle down,

b)                  Rotate it a further 90.

 

4)                   Step (4)

a)                 


Create a new, private secret code and turn the dials to this position (in this demonstration, the secret code is 5-5-5)

b)                  Keep a record of the new combination.

 

5)                   Step (5)

a)                 


Rotate the shackle 90 clockwise backward to the first 90 rotation position.

 

b)                  Slightly pull to raise the shackle

c)                  Turn the shackle further clockwise 90 to its original position.

 

d)                  Close the shackle.

 

e)                  Rotate the dials to a random sequence to disguise your secret code, and lock the device; do not share your code with anyone for the duration of your stay.
