Instructions to create a unique private code
for your personal private door lock

MANUFACTURERS OPERATING INSTRUCTIONS:

(This lock is pre-set at the factory to operate/open at 0-0-0)

Download PDF

Step (1)

  1. Turn dials to 0-0-0 (Factory setting), to open door for first time.

  1. and pull out the shackle:

Step (2)

  1. Turn the shackle counterclockwise 90°.

  1. and press it down:

Step (3)

  1. While holding shackle down,

  1. Rotate it a further 90°.

Step (4)

  1. Create a new, private secret code and turn the dials to this position (in this demonstration, the secret code is 5-5-5)

  1. Keep a record of the new combination.

Step (5)

  1. Rotate the shackle 90 clockwise backward to the first 90 rotation position.

  1. Slightly pull to raise the shackle

  1. Turn the shackle further clockwise 90° to its original position.

  1. Close the shackle.

Step (6)

  1. Rotate the dials to a random sequence to disguise your secret code, and lock the device; do not share your code with anyone for the duration of your stay.

Important Reminder

Please reset combination to 0-0-0 when vacating.
