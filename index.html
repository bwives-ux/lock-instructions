Instructions to create a unique private code
for your personal private door lock

MANUFACTURERS OPERATING INSTRUCTIONS:

(This lock is pre-set at the factory to operate/open at 0-0-0)

Download PDF

Step 1 — Open on the factory code (0-0-0)

  1. Turn dials to 0-0-0 and pull out the shackle.

Step 2 — Rotate and press down

  1. Turn the shackle counterclockwise 90°.
  2. Press it down firmly.

Step 3 — Rotate further

  1. While holding the shackle down, rotate it another 90°.

Step 4 — Set your own code

  1. Turn the dials to your new secret code (e.g., 5-5-5).
  2. Keep a record of your combination.

Step 5 — Return and lock

  1. Rotate the shackle clockwise back to its original position.
  2. Slightly lift the shackle and return it to the first 90° rotation.
  3. Close the shackle completely.
  4. Rotate the dials randomly to lock and conceal your code.

Important Reminder

Please reset the combination to 0-0-0 when vacating.
