Lock Instructions

Step-by-step guide for setting and securing the combination lock.

Before you start

The lock ships from the factory set to 0-0-0. You will open it on 0-0-0 once, then set your own private code and scramble the dials.

Step 1 — Open on the factory code (0-0-0)

  1. Turn dials to 0-0-0 and pull out the shackle.

Step 2 — Press down at 90°

  1. Rotate the shackle counter-clockwise 90° and press it down.

Step 3 — Turn further to set mode

  1. While holding it down, rotate the shackle a further 90°.

Step 4 — Choose your new code

  1. Turn the dials to your new private code. Don’t use 0-0-0.

Tip: Record the new combination somewhere safe.

Step 5 — Return the shackle to normal

  1. Rotate the shackle back clockwise 90° to the first position.
  2. Lift slightly to release, then rotate another 90° back to the original position.
  3. Close the shackle.

Step 6 — Scramble & lock

  1. Rotate the dials to a random sequence to hide your code. Confirm the lock is closed.

Leaving? Please reset the combination to 0-0-0 when vacating.
